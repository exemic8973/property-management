# Product Vision: PropertyOS

## Product Name
**PropertyOS** - Intelligent Property Management Platform

## Executive Summary
PropertyOS is a vertically integrated, AI-powered property management platform that unifies tenants, property owners, and managers in a single, beautiful experience. Built for the 2020s and beyond, it transforms fragmented operations into a data-driven, automated ecosystem that scales from individual landlords to enterprise portfolios.

---

## 1. Target Users

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Profile | Scale | Pain Points | Annual Addressable Market (US) |
|---------|---------|-------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| **Individual Landlords** | 1-10 properties, self-managed | Small | Manual rent collection, scattered spreadsheets, reactive maintenance | $2.4B |
| **Small Property Managers** | 10-100 properties, 2-10 staff | Small-Medium | Disparate tools, owner reporting friction, manual compliance | $4.8B |
| **Mid-Market Firms** | 100-1,000 properties, 10-100 staff | Medium | Data silos, limited automation, operational inefficiency | $6.2B |
| **Enterprise Portfolios** | 1,000+ properties, 100+ staff, multi-state | Large | Legacy systems, compliance complexity, portfolio optimization | $8.5B |

### Secondary Stakeholders
- **Vendors**: Maintenance contractors, cleaning services, inspectors
- **Financial Partners**: Banks, lenders, accounting firms
- **Compliance Bodies**: Housing authorities, regulatory agencies
- **Tenants' Families**: Emergency contacts, guarantors

## 2. Core Value Propositions

### For Tenants
1. **Frictionless Payments**
   - One-click rent payment via ACH, credit card, or digital wallet
   - Autopay scheduling with smart reminders
   - Instant payment confirmation and digital receipts

2. **Transparent Communication**
   - Real-time maintenance request tracking with photo uploads
   - In-app messaging with property management
   - Notification preferences (email, SMS, push)

3. **Digital Lease Management**
   - E-signature integration (DocuSign, HelloSign)
   - Lease versioning with change history
   - Document repository (insurance, notices, policies)

4. **AI Concierge**
   - 24/7 natural language support
   - Instant answers to FAQs (parking, pet policies, amenities)
   - Proactive alerts (upcoming inspections, payment reminders)

### For Property Owners/Investors
1. **Portfolio Intelligence**
   - Real-time cash flow, NOI, ROI visualization
   - Property-level drill-down with granular metrics
   - CapEx tracking with depreciation schedules

2. **Automated Financial Management**
   - Owner statements with distribution scheduling
   - Tax-ready exportable reports (Schedule E, Form 1099)
   - Expense categorization with AI-assisted tagging

3. **Predictive Analytics**
   - Vacancy risk scoring with intervention recommendations
   - Rent pricing optimization using market data
   - Maintenance cost forecasting

4. **Executive Summaries**
   - AI-generated monthly/quarterly performance briefings
   - Customizable KPI dashboards
   - Benchmarking against market comparables

### For Property Managers
1. **Operational Efficiency**
   - Centralized work order management with vendor portal
   - Automated task routing based on skill/location
   - SLA tracking with escalation alerts

2. **Team Orchestration**
   - Role-based access with granular permissions
   - Assignment tracking with performance metrics
   - Automated reminders and follow-ups

3. **Compliance Automation**
   - State-specific lease templates with auto-updates
   - Regulatory deadline tracking (inspections, certifications)
   - Audit-ready documentation trails

4. **Vendor Network**
   - Curated vendor marketplace with ratings
   - Automated bid solicitation for major repairs
   - Invoice processing with approval workflows

4. **Automated Lease Renewals**: AI generates personalized renewal offers with success probability
5. **Multi-Currency Support**: Built-in currency conversion for international investors
6. **Voice Commands**: Alexa/Google Assistant integration for hands-free operations

## 4. Monetization Strategy

### Pricing Tiers

#### Essential (For Individual Landlords)
- **Price**: $29/month + $2/unit/month
- **Units**: 1-25 units
- **Features**: Basic property management, tenant portal, payment processing (2.9% + $0.30), standard reporting
- **AI**: Limited (FAQ bot only)

#### Professional (For Small Property Managers)
- **Price**: $99/month + $1.50/unit/month
- **Units**: 26-200 units
- **Features**: All Essential + work orders, vendor portal, owner portal, advanced analytics
- **AI**: Maintenance triage, payment reminders, basic insights
- **Support**: Email + chat

#### Business (For Mid-Market Firms)
- **Price**: $299/month + $1.00/unit/month
- **Units**: 201-2,000 units
- **Features**: All Professional + custom branding, API access, integrations, priority support
- **AI**: Full suite (revenue optimizer, churn prediction, executive summaries)
- **Support**: Email + chat + phone

#### Enterprise (For Large Portfolios)
- **Price**: Custom (starting at $1,000/month)
- **Units**: 2,000+ units
- **Features**: All Business + dedicated account manager, custom integrations, white-label option, SLA guarantees
- **AI**: Custom models, fine-tuning on portfolio data
- **Support**: 24/7 dedicated success team

### Revenue Streams

1. **Subscription Fees**: Tiered SaaS pricing (70% of revenue)
2. **Payment Processing**: Stripe partnership with 0.25% revenue share (15% of revenue)
3. **Marketplace Fees**: 5% commission on vendor transactions (8% of revenue)
4. **Enterprise Services**: Custom development, consulting, training (5% of revenue)
5. **Data Monetization**: Aggregated market insights (anonymized) sold to institutional investors (2% of revenue)

### Key Metrics
- **Target CAC**: $150 (Essential), $500 (Professional), $2,000 (Business)
- **Target LTV**: $2,700 (3-year), $18,000 (3-year), $108,000 (3-year)
- **Target LTV:CAC**: 18:1 (Essential), 36:1 (Professional), 54:1 (Business)
- **Gross Margin**: 85% (excluding payment processing)
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 120%+

### Go-to-Market Strategy

#### Phase 1: Self-Service (Months 1-12)
- Product-led growth with free 14-day trial
- Content marketing (SEO, guides, webinars)
- Marketplace listings (Capterra, G2, Software Advice)

#### Phase 2: Direct Sales (Months 6-24)
- Outbound SDR team targeting 100+ unit managers
- Partnerships with real estate associations (NAR, NAA)
- Referral program with 20% recurring commission

#### Phase 3: Enterprise (Months 18-36)
- Dedicated enterprise sales team
- Strategic partnerships with property management franchises
- White-label offering for large REITs

## 8. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Data Breach** | Critical | Medium | SOC 2 compliance, encryption, bug bounty, penetration testing |
| **Regulatory Changes** | High | High | Legal counsel, compliance engine, state-specific templates |
| **Competition from Incumbents** | High | Medium | First-mover on AI, superior UX, faster iteration cycles |
| **Payment Processing Disruption** | High | Low | Multiple payment gateway backup, transparent communication |
| **AI Hallucinations** | Medium | Medium | Human-in-the-loop, confidence scores, clear attribution |
| **Customer Churn** | Medium | Medium | High-touch onboarding, success team, feature parity requests |
